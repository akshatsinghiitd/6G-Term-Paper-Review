# A Critical Review of 6G Use Cases and Challenges

This repository contains the term paper submitted as part of the M.Tech (Communication Engineering) coursework at IIT Delhi.

## Abstract
The term paper presents a critical review of emerging 6G technologies and proposed use cases, focusing on both their potential and practical limitations. The study evaluates recent research trends and highlights challenges related to propagation, hardware feasibility, energy efficiency, and large-scale deployment.

## Topics Covered
- Terahertz (THz) Communication
- Cell-Free Massive MIMO
- Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS)
- Li-Fi and IoT Integration
- AI-Native and Semantic Communication
- Energy Efficiency and Deployment Challenges in 6G

## Key Takeaways
- Identified gaps between theoretical 6G promises and real-world feasibility.
- Analyzed hardware and propagation constraints using recent IEEE literature.
- Discussed the role of semantic and goal-oriented communication in future networks.
